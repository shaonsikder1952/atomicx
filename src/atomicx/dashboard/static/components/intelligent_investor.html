Key Lessons from The Intelligent Investor
1
Investing vs Speculating
Investing means buying based on value. Speculating means betting on price moves. We focus on investing.
2
Ignore Market Noise
Daily price swings don't matter if fundamentals are sound. Focus on long-term value, not short-term volatility.
3
Margin of Safety
Always buy with a cushion. If something is worth $100, don't pay $100 - pay $85 or less.
4
Defensive Investing
Protect your capital first. Don't chase high returns if it means taking excessive risk.
